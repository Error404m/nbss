Message

My Dear Well Wishers,

Welcome to the corridors of NBSS Shukulpur. Our CBSE Board English Medium School, situated in the heart of Shukulpur with a spacious campus, stands as a beacon of academic excellence, skill development, and character formation since its founding in 2011.

The NBSS family — with our efficient and committed staff — ensures that every child who enters our campus grows fully into a fine human being. We envision education that is self-directed, individualized, experience-based, and growth-oriented.

Students at NBSS have ample opportunity to prove their mettle across curricular and co-curricular activities. Discipline is the backbone of NBSS, while the spirit of national integration is kept alive through our four houses, festivals, and competitions.

  • Parent-teacher meetings held every month for continuous interaction
  • Remedial classes arranged for weaker learners to ensure no child is left behind
  • Regular orientation programmes for staff members to enhance teaching effectiveness

Admissions 2024-25

Application for Admission
in Session 2024-25

Seats are limited. Apply early to secure your child's place at NBSS Shukulpur.

  • The guardian responsible for the child must personally introduce every candidate to the Principal for admission.
  • New admissions are subject to availability of seats. Admission is strictly on merit basis.
  • Students from recognized schools must present the original Leaving/Transfer Certificate.
  • Transfer Certificates from outside the state must be countersigned by the state's inspecting authority.
  • No special references or recommendations will be entertained. Admissions are through proper channels only.
  • The school management reserves the right to admit or retain students in the school.
