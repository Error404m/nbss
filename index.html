Message from Principal Mam's Desk

Dear Parents, Students, and Staff,

As we embark on another exciting week at NBSS's School Lucknow, I extend a warm welcome to each of you. It's my privilege to address you from my desk as we navigate through the academic year with enthusiasm and dedication.

This month promises an array of enriching experiences, both inside and outside the classroom. Our talented faculty remains committed to nurturing the intellectual curiosity and personal growth of every student. Through innovative teaching methodologies and holistic development initiatives, we aim to empower our students to excel in all facets of life.

  • Parent teacher meeting is held in every month for a vivid interaction with parents and teachers.
  • Remedial classes are arranged for the weaker / slow learners.
  • Orientation programmes are held for the staff members to be more effective in guiding their wards.

Let's continue to work together to create a nurturing environment where every individual can thrive and succeed. Thank you for your continued support and involvement in our journey of educational excellence.


NBSS Dwivedi
Principal

Email: NBSS@NBSS.com
