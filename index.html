Message from Principal's Desk

My Dear Well Wishers,

Welcome to the corridors of NBSS Shukulpur to find the wards making their best, in school life. NBSS, Shukulpur , CBSE Board English Medium School, is situated in the heart of Shukulpur with a spacious campus. The School, in 2011. The School stands for academic excellence, development of skills and character formation. NBSS family with her efficient & committed staff sees that every child, who enters the campus grows fully to a fine human being, It ia agreat pleasure to record that the dream establishment of NBSS is envisioned to discover education different from current methodolgy to be more self- directed , individualized , interceptive, experience-based and injury-oriented for example.

The students, who pass out from the portals of N.B.S.S. , shine in every field outside, as they are strongly founded on the value based quality education. Students at NBSS have ample opportunity to prove their mettle in bringing out the best of talents in them. Success and prosperity is possible, only when the things are done in a disciplined manner. Thus discipline is the backbone of NBSS’ School. The whole school is divided into four houses, the staff as animators to help out the students to stand tall in taking up responsibility for the welfare of the school and of every individual. Various competitions, celebration of major festivals of all religions and National festivals, keep the inmates in the spirit of national integration.The school has two terms -- April to September & October to March with one unit test before the half yearly and one before the final examination to bring out the best of results in their respective classes and the board examination.

  • Parent teacher meeting is held in every month for a vivid interaction with parents and teachers.
  • Remedial classes are arranged for the weaker / slow learners.
  • Orientation programmes are held for the staff members to be more effective in guiding their wards.

Smt. Mamta Tripathi,Chairman of the School and Mr. Ajay Shukla, Manager of the school are guiding to achieve the highest excellence in both curricular and co-curricular activities, thanks to their regular and timely guidance and support.


Mr. Anuj Shukla
